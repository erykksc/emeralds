The goal of the game is to collect as many gems as you can.
In order to do so you have to explore the cave.
In the cave you can find gems, relicts and traps.
If you find gems the gems will be split among 
the players that explore and the reminder will stay in place.
If you find relict it will stay in place.
If you find the same type of trap 2 times you will lose all 
the gems from this round.
You can also go back to save the gems you have collected 
in a round and collect all the gems that were left behind
(if more players will go back at the same time the gems will 
also be split among them).
If only one player will go back he will collect all the relicts
and thus will earn as many gems as the relicts are worth.
